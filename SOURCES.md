## Translation Notes

All quotes are presented in English translation. Multiple English translations of *Go Rin no Sho* exist (notably by Victor Harris, 1974; Thomas Cleary, 1993; William Scott Wilson, 2002). The phrasing used here draws from commonly circulated translations and may not correspond word-for-word to any single published edition.

Zen quotes from *Shobogenzo* primarily follow translations by Kazuaki Tanahashi and the San Francisco Zen Center.

## Note on "Composed" Quotes

Quotes labeled `composed` are original writing created in the spirit of their respective traditions. They are not attributed to any historical figure and should not be cited as historical quotations.
